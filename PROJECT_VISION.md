## üìâ The Problem: "Dumb" Traffic Lights
Most traffic lights today operate on fixed timers. Whether it's a busy Monday morning or a quiet Sunday night, they follow the same pattern. This leads to:
*   **Wasted Time:** You sit at a red light while the green lane is empty.
*   **Pollution:** Constant idling and stop-and-go traffic increase carbon emissions.
*   **Emergency Delays:** Ambulances and fire trucks get stuck behind hundreds of cars.
*   **Economic Loss:** Thousands of hours are lost in traffic every day.

## üöÄ Our Solution: The AI Traffic Controller
Instead of a simple timer, we use **Deep Reinforcement Learning**‚Äîthe same technology that allows AI to beat grandmasters at Chess or Go. 

### How it works (Simplified):
1.  **Real Data:** We use actual traffic data from Bangalore's most notorious junctions.
2.  **The "Duel":** We run two simulations side-by-side. 
    *   **Side A (The Past):** Uses standard fixed timers.
    *   **Side B (The Future):** Controlled by our AI.
3.  **Learning to Move:** The AI earns "points" for every vehicle that clears the junction and gets "penalized" for long queues. Over time, it learns the perfect rhythm for any traffic density.
4.  **Dynamic Adaptation:** The AI automatically detects when it's raining, when it's a peak hour, or when an ambulance is approaching, and adjusts the signals instantly.
